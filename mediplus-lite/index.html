General consultation

Patients can schedule appointments with general practitioners or family physicians for routine check-ups, preventive care, or management of common health concerns. Appointments can be made through the hospital's online booking system or by contacting the hospital directly.

Obstetrics Consultation

Expectant mothers can access specialized obstetric care by scheduling appointments with obstetricians or maternal-fetal medicine specialists. These consultations involve comprehensive assessments of maternal and fetal health, including prenatal screenings, ultrasounds, and personalized care plans.

Pro - Pharmacist

Iniesat Hospital offers consultations with experienced pharmacists who provide personalized medication management services. Patients can schedule appointments to discuss medication-related concerns, including drug interactions, side effects, and adherence strategies.

Minor Surgeries

Patients requiring minor surgical procedures can undergo these treatments at Iniesat Hospital's surgical facilities. Procedures are performed by skilled surgeons in a safe and sterile environment, with pre-operative assessments and post-operative care provided by the hospital's medical team.

Prenatal and Postnatal Care

Iniesat Hospital offers comprehensive prenatal and postnatal care programs to support expectant mothers throughout their pregnancy journey. This includes regular check-ups, prenatal screenings, childbirth education classes, breastfeeding support, and postpartum follow-up care.

Delivery

Iniesat Hospital provides state-of-the-art labor and delivery services in modern maternity wards equipped with advanced medical technology. Expectant mothers receive personalized care during labor, with access to pain management options, obstetric interventions, and skilled medical support from obstetricians, midwives, and nurses.
